About Us

The Energy Research Institute @ NTU (ERI@N) was formed by Nanyang Technological University (NTU) to spearhead the University’s efforts in the area of sustainable energy research. ERI@N will focus on the areas of sustainable energy, energy efficiency/infrastructure and socio-economic aspects of energy research.
